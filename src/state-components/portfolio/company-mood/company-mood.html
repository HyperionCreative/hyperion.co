Each page has a clear purpose

While the home page serves to introduce visitors to the product and convince them it is worthy of their time, we also created more detailed pages that explain Company Mood’s pricing model, their features or the company’s history, and we think this structure will give Company Mood’s visitors all the information they need to understand their product and sign up.

Understanding the Client

We started this redesign project, by getting to know the client and understand their business, their market, their product and of course their user base.

Project Goals

The goal of the project was to create a website with a professional and trustworthy look that is structured to better explain how Company Mood’s product works, how much it costs and what advantages it can offer to users. The website had to cater to a technical audience of entrepreneurs and managers of medium sized firms, it had to be responsive and support multiple languages.

Defining the Structure

Once we had a clear picture of Company Mood’s message, we started exploring ways in which this information could be communicated to users and eventually defined the site’s structure.
